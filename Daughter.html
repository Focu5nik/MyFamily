Sister

Maybe my sister is not the funniest, the greatest, or not the perfect, but she is the one whom I can blindly trust. She is the one who can easily make me laugh. She is the one who can effortlessly make me feel great. And that’s one of the many reasons why every sister has been really important in our life. Let me share a few words about my elder sister today.

She is an amazing human being in my life. It’s impossible to complete writing about her in a few words. One of the cutest things about her and me, we always keep fighting. She is older than me, but being the youngest, I win the fight always. But we never hit each other.
